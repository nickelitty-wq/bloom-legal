Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Bloom - Daily Affirmations respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Bloom does not collect personally identifiable information directly. Most app data is stored locally on your device.

This locally stored data may include:

  • Affirmation preferences and favorites
  • Streak and usage data
  • Notification settings
  • Onboarding responses

Subscription and Payment Data

Bloom uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat and Apple may collect certain information required to process payments, such as purchase history, device identifiers, and subscription status.

Bloom does not have access to your full payment details. All transactions are handled securely through the App Store.

You can review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy here: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Permissions

Bloom may request permission to send notifications to deliver your daily affirmation reminders. These permissions can be managed in your device settings at any time.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Most app data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Your Rights and Choices

You may control, update, or delete your locally stored data by managing the app settings or uninstalling the app.

For subscription-related data, you may manage your information through your Apple ID account settings.

Children's Privacy

Bloom is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
